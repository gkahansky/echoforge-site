Got Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does turnaround actually take? expand_more
Podcast content packages are delivered within 24 hours of upload. Marketing audit PDFs are also delivered within 24 hours. Free sample outputs are faster: podcast samples in 5–10 minutes, audit samples in 10–20 minutes.
What file formats are supported for podcast upload? expand_more
We accept MP3, MP4, M4A, WAV, and WEBM files up to 200 MB. The free sample accepts up to 10 minutes of audio. Paid orders support full episodes up to one hour.
How does the free sample work? expand_more
For the Podcast Studio: upload up to 10 minutes of audio and enter your email. You'll receive a real watermarked sample output — not a demo — in 5–10 minutes. For the Marketing Audit: enter your URL and email. We review your live site and email you a censored PDF showing your overall score and dimension breakdown. No credit card required for either.
How do you ensure quality? expand_more
EchoForge combines structured workflows, automation, and review logic to produce clean, publication-ready deliverables. The free sample lets you verify quality before purchasing, and the paid audits are designed to give you clear next steps rather than generic output.
Do you offer accessibility audits? expand_more
Yes. You can start with a free accessibility sample report, order a 5-page WCAG audit for $120, or book a premium full-site accessibility report for $250. Premium marketing audits also include accessibility coverage as a bundled value-add.
What is your refund policy? expand_more
If the output doesn't meet the quality standard described, contact us within 7 days and we'll reprocess or refund. We offer a free sample specifically so you can evaluate quality before committing.
Is my audio or website data kept private? expand_more
Your uploaded files and website data are processed solely to generate your content package or audit. We do not share, sell, or retain your data beyond what's needed for delivery. Files are deleted after processing.
